How to Play

  • Select a programming language and difficulty level
  • Click "Start Test" or press Ctrl+Enter to begin
  • Type the code snippet as accurately and quickly as possible
  • Your score is based on speed, accuracy, and combo multipliers
  • Earn achievements and level up as you progress

Scoring System

  • +1 point per correct character
  • Combo bonus up to 5x points for long streaks
  • -5 points for incorrect characters (breaks combo)
  • WPM (Words Per Minute) calculated based on 5-character words
  • Star rating based on final score (500+ = 1 star, 3000+ = 5 stars)
